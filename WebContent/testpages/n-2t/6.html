Motorcycles

A motorcycle (also called a moto) is a two or three wheeled motor vehicle

There are around 200 million motorcycles in use worldwide

There are about 73 motorcycles per 1000 people

Most of the motorcycles, 63%, are in the developing countries of Asia

As of 2002, India, with an estimated 37 million motorcycles/mopeds, was home to the largest number of

motorized two wheelers in the world.
