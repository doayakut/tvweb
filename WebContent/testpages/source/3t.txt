Antipodes Water Company is a New Zealand water-bottling company.

Mountain Valley Spring Water
Mountain Valley Spring Water is bottled in Hot Springs, Arkansas.
It has been bottled continuously since 1871.
Mountain Valley Spring Water is currently owned by Clear Mountain Spring Water Company of Little Rock, Arkansas.
