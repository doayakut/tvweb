Types of Fabric

Kuba velvet

A cloth made by the Kuba people of the Democratic Republic of Congo from raffia is often referred to as " Kuba velvet". More recently, synthetic velvets have been developed, mostly polyester, nylon, viscose, acetate, and mixtures of different synthetics, or synthetics and natural fibers (for example viscose mixed with silk). A small percentage of spandex is sometimes added to give stretch. raditionally, velvet is associated with nobility
