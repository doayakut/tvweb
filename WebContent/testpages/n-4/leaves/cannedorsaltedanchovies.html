Types of Fish

Canned or salted anchovies

Canned or salted anchovies have a pronounced, salty tang; fresh anchovies have a rich but subtle taste and a soft texture. The skin is edible. Fresh anchovies are uncommon, and it’s hard to find unbruised, whole fish, since they are so delicate.
