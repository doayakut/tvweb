Visitor catches record lingcod . A 20-year old angler on his first-ever fishing trip to Alaska caught a world-record lingcod Friday about 80 miles south of Ninilchik. The large female, caught by Robbie Hammond from Discovery Bay, Calif., weighed 82.6 pounds and measured 55 inches long. It beat the old record fish, caught in 2002 by Charles Curney, by just one pound.
