Brands of bottled water

Fiji

Fiji water comes from an artesian aquifer in the Yaqara Valley of Viti Levu.

The company is owned by Resnick family.

Fiji waters was started in 1996 by David Gilmour.

The pH of Fiji waters is 7.9.

In December 2010, Fiji Water's Fiji plant had 400 employees.
