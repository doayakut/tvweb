Brands of bottled water

Dasani

The first source of Dasani water was in Alberta.

Dasani is owned by The Coca-Cola Company.

The company was launched in 1999, after the success of Aquafina.

The Dasani brand includes flavored water; lemon, grape, raspberry, and strawberry.

Dasani was introduced to the Brazilian market in mid-2003.
